Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,Cranberries,and Brie

Wow your taste bubs this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions,Tart cranbellies,and creamy brie.it will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

FEATURED IN:
fresh cranberry recipes that go beyond sauce

Incredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra vingin olive oil,plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions,thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil,for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie,cut into cubes(scrape rind of with a paring knife if desired,though its not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh orfrozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl,or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment,mix togheter all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.You dont need to actually knead the dough,though i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
